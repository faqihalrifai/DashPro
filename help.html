Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password from the login page by clicking on "Forgot Password" or in your user profile settings if you are logged in. For security reasons, please follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

Where can I find my order history?

Your complete order history is available under the "Orders" section in the sidebar menu. You can filter by date, status, or search for specific order IDs.

Is this dashboard mobile-friendly?

Yes, DashPro is designed to be fully responsive and works seamlessly on various screen sizes, including mobile phones and tablets. The layout adapts automatically for optimal viewing.

How to add a new product?

Navigate to the "Products" page from the sidebar. Click on the "Add Product" button usually located at the top right of the product list. Fill in the required details such as name, category, price, stock, and upload relevant images. Click 'Save' to add the product.

Can I customize the dashboard colors?

Primary color customization can be done via the theme settings icon in the header. For advanced customization, direct modification of CSS variables in the `style.css` file is recommended.

How do I integrate with third-party services?

Integrations with services like Google Analytics or Mailchimp can be managed from the 'Settings' page under the 'Integrations' tab. You will typically need to provide API keys or tracking IDs from the respective service.
